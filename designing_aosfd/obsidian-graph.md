  ## Obsidian Graph Answer                                                                                                             
                                                            
  Open **just `llm-wiki/`** as the vault — not the parent folder. This keeps the graph clean (only wiki pages, no source code).        
                                                            
  **How:**                                                                                                                             
  1. Obsidian → `File → Open Vault → Open folder as vault`  
  2. Select `llm-wiki/`                                                                                                                
  3. Press `Cmd+G` (Mac) or `Ctrl+G` (Windows) for the graph
                                                                                                                                       
  The graph will be populated because every page we create has `[[wikilinks]]`. The denser the links, the richer the graph. With the   
  bidirectional linking rule in place, your graph will look like an actual knowledge web — hub pages (like `entities/comms-engine`)    
  will have many connections, concept pages will cluster around their domain.
